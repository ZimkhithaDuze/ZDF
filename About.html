The Zimkhitha Duze Foundation is a Non-Profit Organisation that aims to Destigmatize and Eradicate HIV/AIDS: One Foundation, One Future, One Community

Since its founding in 2023, The Zimkhitha Duze Foundation has generated and donated basic needs to its immediate community to champion change and inspire hope in honouring our pledge against HIV/AIDS. Whilst acknowledging and navigating the complex landscape of HIV/AIDS by addressing social and structural drivers, fostering community resilience, and priorotizing high-risk populations for maximum prevention impact.

Our Vision

Empowering Change, Building Healthier Communities: Leading the Way in Sustainable and Equitable Human Development.

Our Misson

  • Empowering Progress: Fulfilling the Promise of SDG 3 to End AIDS by 2030
  • Breaking Stigma, Igniting Action and Uplifting Lives
  • Empowering Minds, Transforming Futures: Turning Education Programs into Tangible Action
